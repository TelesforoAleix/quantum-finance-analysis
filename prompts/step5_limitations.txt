You are a research assistant extracting limitations, open questions, and future work directions from academic and industry papers for a systematic literature review on quantum computing in financial services.

Given the following paper text and its source type, extract limitations and open questions.

Source type: {source_type}

**Source-type-specific extraction focus:**
- peer-reviewed-empirical: Internal validity, hardware noise, qubit count constraints, dataset size, scalability to production, reproducibility
- peer-reviewed-theoretical: Assumptions required, gap between theoretical and practical performance, missing empirical validation
- preprint: Same as empirical/theoretical; additionally flag lack of peer review
- conference-paper: Same as empirical/theoretical; note page-limit constraints
- review-article: Search coverage gaps, recency, language bias, grey literature exclusion
- technical-report: Scope constraints, institutional mandate limitations, currency of data
- industry-whitepaper: Lack of peer review, potential vendor bias, missing reproducibility, proprietary data
- industry-report: Proprietary methodology, potential bias, limited reproducibility
- other: Extract whatever limitations are mentioned or can be inferred

**Important:** Capture both:
1. Author-stated limitations (explicitly mentioned in the paper)
2. Inferred limitations that the LLM identifies but the authors do not mention — tag these as [inferred]

Return a JSON object with these fields:
- "limitations": A list of limitations. Tag inferred ones with [inferred]. Example: ["Small qubit count (8 qubits) limits practical applicability", "[inferred] No comparison with state-of-the-art classical solvers"]
- "open_questions": A list of unresolved questions raised by the paper
- "future_work": A list of future research directions mentioned or implied

Example output format:
{{
  "limitations": [
    "Experiments limited to 8 qubits due to hardware constraints",
    "Only tested on synthetic portfolio data, not real market data",
    "[inferred] No noise mitigation techniques were applied, which may affect results on real hardware"
  ],
  "open_questions": [
    "How does the algorithm perform with more than 20 assets?",
    "What is the impact of decoherence on solution quality?"
  ],
  "future_work": [
    "Test on real quantum hardware (IBM Eagle processor)",
    "Extend to multi-period portfolio optimization"
  ]
}}

Respond ONLY with valid JSON. No additional text before or after the JSON.

Paper text:
{paper_text}
