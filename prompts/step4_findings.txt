You are a research assistant extracting findings from academic and industry papers for a systematic literature review on quantum computing in financial services.

Given the following paper text and its source type, extract the key findings and claims.

Source type: {source_type}

**Source-type-specific extraction focus:**
- peer-reviewed-empirical: Quantified results with confidence intervals if available. Flag quantum advantage claims. Note whether results are from simulation or real hardware
- peer-reviewed-theoretical: Key propositions or theorems, claimed complexity advantages, conditions under which claims hold
- preprint: Default quantum advantage claims to [speculative] unless strong empirical support is present
- conference-paper: Same as empirical/theoretical
- review-article: Key themes identified, consensus findings, areas of disagreement across reviewed literature
- technical-report: Recommendations, technical conclusions, policy implications
- industry-whitepaper: Claims made. Default all to [speculative] unless backed by cited peer-reviewed evidence. Note vendor context
- industry-report: Market findings, trend claims, projections. Note proprietary data context
- other: Extract whatever findings are present

**Inline claim tagging rules:**
Every claim MUST be tagged with one of these inline markers:
- [supported] — claim backed by empirical results in this paper
- [speculative] — argued theoretically, not empirically validated
- [disputed] — contradicts findings in other known literature

Return a JSON object with these fields:
- "key_findings": A list of findings, each tagged with [supported], [speculative], or [disputed]. Example: ["[supported] QAOA achieves 95% accuracy on 8-qubit portfolio optimization", "[speculative] Quantum advantage may emerge at 100+ qubits"]
- "results_summary": A paragraph summarizing the main results
- "performance_claims": A list of specific performance claims with numbers/metrics. Empty list if none
- "quantum_advantage_claim": One of: "demonstrated", "theoretical", "speculative", "disputed", "not-applicable"
- "quantum_advantage_detail": Brief explanation of the quantum advantage claim classification

Example output format:
{{
  "key_findings": [
    "[supported] The hybrid quantum-classical algorithm solved the portfolio optimization problem with 95% accuracy",
    "[speculative] The authors suggest this approach could scale to 1000-asset portfolios"
  ],
  "results_summary": "The paper demonstrates that...",
  "performance_claims": [
    "95% accuracy on 8-qubit optimization",
    "2x speedup over classical simulated annealing on 6-asset portfolios"
  ],
  "quantum_advantage_claim": "speculative",
  "quantum_advantage_detail": "Results are from simulation only; claimed advantage not demonstrated on real hardware"
}}

Respond ONLY with valid JSON. No additional text before or after the JSON.

Paper text:
{paper_text}
